Questions

Good to know before you call.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Forge Cleaning & Property Services is fully licensed and insured, so contractors, property managers, and homeowners can bring us onto a property with confidence.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Jacksonville and serve the greater metro, including Jacksonville Beach, Ponte Vedra, Orange Park, Mandarin, St. Johns, Fleming Island, Fernandina Beach, Nocatee, and Yulee. If you're not sure whether you're in our area, just call us at (904) 469-7439.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes — every quote is free, and there's no obligation. Send the form or call us with your property type and what you need, and we'll get back to you fast with a clear price.

Do you handle commercial and residential work?

Both. We work with contractors, property managers, offices, and businesses, as well as homeowners who need pressure washing, painting, lanai and pool deck cleaning, and more. If it involves cleaning or property services, ask us.

How does scheduling work?

Once we confirm scope and price, we lock in a date that works for you — including recurring schedules for offices and commercial spaces. We show up when we say we will, so you can plan around us.

How does pricing and payment work?

Every job is quoted based on the property and scope, so you get a fair, specific price rather than a one-size-fits-all rate. We'll walk you through the details and accepted payment methods when we confirm your quote.
